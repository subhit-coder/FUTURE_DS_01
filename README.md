🚀 Project Overview

The Business Sales Performance Dashboard provides insights into key business metrics such as:

Total Revenue

Country-wise Revenue Distribution

Product Sales Performance

Monthly Sales Trends

Day-wise Sales Analysis

This dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand business performance, top-selling products, and revenue-generating markets.

🎯 Objectives

The primary goals of this project are:

Analyze overall business revenue performance

Identify top-performing products

Track monthly revenue trends

Understand regional sales distribution

Provide interactive filtering for deeper analysis

📊 Key Dashboard Insights
💰 Total Revenue

Displays the total revenue generated across the dataset.

🌍 Top 5 Countries by Revenue

Shows the countries contributing the most to overall revenue.

📦 Top 10 Products by Revenue

Identifies the products generating the highest sales revenue.

📈 Top 5 Products by Quantity

Highlights products with the highest units sold.

📅 Revenue This Month

Displays revenue generated in the selected month.

🗓️ Sales by Day of the Week

Shows which days have the highest sales activity.

🔎 Interactive Filters

Users can filter the dashboard by:

Year

Month
